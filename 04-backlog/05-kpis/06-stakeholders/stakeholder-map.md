## Stakeholder Map
The digital lending product requires close collaboration across business,
risk, and technology teams to ensure customer value, compliance, and delivery quality.


## Key Stakeholders

Risk Management
- Defines credit policies and risk appetite
- Reviews eligibility and approval logic

Legal & Compliance
- Ensures compliance with KYC, AML, and data protection regulations
- Reviews customer-facing disclosures

IT / Development Squad
- Implements product features and integrations
- Advises on technical feasibility and dependencies

Operations
- Manages manual reviews and exception handling
- Supports post-approval processes

Marketing
- Drives customer acquisition and campaigns
- Optimizes conversion through messaging and channels

Customer Support
- Collects customer feedback and pain points
- Escalates recurring issues for product improvement
