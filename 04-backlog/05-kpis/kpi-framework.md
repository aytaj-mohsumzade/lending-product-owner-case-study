Product Success Metrics

This section outlines how success of the digital lending product is measured,
focusing on speed, customer experience, and operational efficiency.


North Star Metric
Time to decision combined with application completion rate.


Core KPIs
- Average time to credit decision (minutes)
- Loan application completion rate (%)
- Drop-off rate per application step (%)
- Loan approval rate (%)
- Customer satisfaction after decision (NPS)


Usage
KPIs are reviewed regularly to identify bottlenecks, inform backlog prioritization,
and support continuous product improvement.
